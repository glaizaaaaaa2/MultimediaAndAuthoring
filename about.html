About This Site

Context

This website serves as a digital portfolio for the Multimedia and Authoring course, documenting the creative journey through two major projects: Rotoscoping and 3D Rendering.

The rotoscoping project involved splitting reference footage into 5-second clips, rendering them through After Effects, then tracing and editing each frame in Photoshop.

The 3D rendering project built a realistic model of the solar system — exploring lighting, texturing, orbital mechanics, and perspective.

Together, these projects reflect a growing proficiency in digital media authoring and a genuine curiosity for the intersection of art and technology.
